ğŸš¨ EMERGENCY

Meltdown

  • Stay calm
  • Reduce demands
  • "I'm here. Take your time."
  • Offer choices
  • When calm: "What can I do to help?"

Refusing Work

  • Don't argue
  • "Let's try this together"
  • Break it down
  • Offer alternatives
  • Check basic needs

ğŸ¯ QUICK FIXES

Can't Focus

  • Move closer to teacher
  • Fidget tools
  • Movement break
  • Reduce distractions
  • Check if hungry/tired

Overwhelmed

  • Break into steps
  • Offer choices
  • Use timer
  • Encourage
  • Check understanding

Acting Out

  • Stay calm
  • Use proximity
  • Redirect positively
  • Offer alternatives
  • Document

Giving Up

  • Acknowledge effort
  • Adjust difficulty
  • Offer support
  • Celebrate small wins
  • Check understanding
