üö® EMERGENCY


Meltdown


- Stay calm


- Reduce demands


- "I'm here. Take your time."


- Offer choices


- When calm: "What can I do to help?"


Refusing Work


- Don't argue


- "Let's try this together"


- Break it down


- Offer alternatives


- Check basic needs
