## 💡 Tips for Using These Flowcharts

### **How to Use**
- **Follow the process** step by step
- **Use decision trees** to make choices
- **Reference flowcharts** when stuck
- **Print and post** for team reference
- **Customize** for your specific needs

### **Best Practices**
- **Start with research** before writing
- **Build relationships** early and often
- **Follow funder guidelines** exactly
- **Use clear, compelling language**
- **Include specific data and stories**
- **Plan for sustainability**
